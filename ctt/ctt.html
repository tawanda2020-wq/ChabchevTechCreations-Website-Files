What is Chabchev Tales & Tasks?

Chabchev Tales & Tasks is a creative literary brand that showcases and manages independently published works by authors with a passion for storytelling, reflection, and learning. Currently led by its founder, Tawanda Ronald Munyanyi, it offers an evolving collection of content—from novels and poems to logbooks, journals, puzzles, and works that can only be expressed on paper.

We do not operate as a traditional publishing house, but rather a creative platform that oversees, distributes, and presents written works to the world—mostly via self-publishing platforms like Amazon KDP. Our mission is to bring authentic, thoughtful content to readers who appreciate quality craftsmanship in literature.

Meet the Creator

Tawanda Ronald Munyanyi

Tawanda is a passionate writer, creator, and storyteller who believes in the transformative power of words. With a background in diverse fields and a love for authentic expression, he creates works that bridge the gap between entertainment and education, always striving to offer readers something meaningful and memorable.

When not writing, Tawanda enjoys exploring new ideas, solving puzzles, and connecting with fellow creatives who share his vision of making literature accessible and impactful.
